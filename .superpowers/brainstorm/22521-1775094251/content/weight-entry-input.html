A. Stepper (+/−)

Defaults to yesterday's weight. Tap +/− to adjust. Toggle step size (0.1 / 0.5 / 1.0 lb). Fewest taps when weight is close to yesterday — which it almost always is.

B. Numeric Keypad

Type the full weight each time. No ambiguity — you see exactly what you're logging. More taps than stepper (5-6 vs 1-3), but very explicit. Could pre-fill yesterday's value for quick confirm.

C. Scroll Wheel

iOS-native picker feel. Two wheels — whole pounds + decimal. Starts at yesterday's weight. Tactile, satisfying scroll. Natural on iOS, but slightly slower than a stepper for small adjustments.
