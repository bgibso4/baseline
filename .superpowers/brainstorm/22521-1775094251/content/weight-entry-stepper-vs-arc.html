D. Slider Arc

Drag the handle along an arc to set weight. Range auto-centers around your recent weights (e.g., 190–205). Small +/− buttons for fine 0.1 lb adjustments. Visually striking, satisfying gesture, but more complex interaction than a stepper.

E. Hybrid — Arc Display + Stepper

The arc is a visual indicator showing where your weight sits in your recent range — but you don't drag it. You adjust with the stepper buttons below. Best of both: the visual context of the arc + the simplicity of tap-to-adjust. Arc animates as you tap.
