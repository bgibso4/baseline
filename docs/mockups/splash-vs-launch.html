Splash vs Launch — comparison mockup

Splash is what iOS shows instantly while the app loads — it's a static LaunchScreen.storyboard rendered by the OS before any Swift runs. It cannot animate. Launch animation is a SwiftUI view shown right after, replaying the EKG draw-in before crossfading to the Now tab. Together they hand the user from "tap" → "live app" in one breath.
