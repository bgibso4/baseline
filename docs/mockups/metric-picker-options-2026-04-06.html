Metric Picker + Compare — 3 Options

The challenge: 20+ InBody metrics need to fit, compare toggle needs to stay accessible, and the primary interaction (tapping the chip at the top) needs to feel natural. Each option shows compare OFF and compare ON states.

Option A · Scrollable Overlay Dropdown
Compare OFF
Same pattern as approved mockup. Dropdown overlays content from the chip. Scrollable with max-height. Compare toggle pinned at top of dropdown (not bottom).
+ Spatial connection: chip → dropdown right below it
+ Familiar pattern (iOS menus)
+ Compare toggle always visible at top
− With 20 metrics, dropdown covers most of the screen
− Overlay on dark-on-dark can feel heavy

Option B · Bottom Sheet
Compare OFF
Sheet slides up from bottom. Compare toggle is sticky at the top of the sheet. Metric list scrolls below. Tapping chip triggers the sheet.
+ Infinite scroll room for metrics
+ Compare toggle always visible (sticky header)
+ Native iOS sheet pattern (familiar gesture: swipe down to dismiss)
− Spatial disconnect: chip at top, sheet from bottom
− Heavier transition than a dropdown
− Covers chart/hero content
